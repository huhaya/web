The daily life of a college student

Eating

This is a basic human need, and the school food is okay, but I will occasionally miss the food at home. When I go to college, I occasionally indulge in my diet (actually, it is quite often).

Sleeping

Sleep is also a basic human need, which occupies a large part of the day, but after going to college, it becomes more and more difficult to maintain a regular schedule

Attending classes

Classes also occupy almost all of the working day, and every day I wake up and come to class, take a lunch break after class, and then come back to class, and the day passes before I know it

Having fun

Play is also important, it keeps me in better shape, I can't learn all the time, and play allows me to release stress.

About having fun：
